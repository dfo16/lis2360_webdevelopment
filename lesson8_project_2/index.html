Ponzi Scheme:New Backdrop for Old Crime

Additionally, this peer to peer electronic cash system has brought in a new arena for Ponzi and pyramid schemes to take place. In a recent case, SEC v. Shavers, the promoter of the alleged scheme promised investors that they would receive 7% or more in interest and that their investment would help raise the companys profits.

Pyramid Scheme: New Backdrop for Old Crime

Furthermore, the terms of Ponzi and pyramid schemes are not new in connection with cryptocurrency. Many heads of traditional financial institutions like JP Morgan, Wells Fargo, and DBS Group have dubbed cryptocurrency in general as “a huge Ponzi or Pyramid scheme.”
